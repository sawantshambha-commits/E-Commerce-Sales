\## 💳 Payment Insights

\- Payment success rate is high but still has room for improvement.

\- UPI / Card are the most used payment methods.

\## 🚀 Recommendations

\- Focus on retaining high-value customers through offers or loyalty programs.

\- Reduce order cancellations by improving delivery and service quality.
